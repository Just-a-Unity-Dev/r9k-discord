This is a recreation of the classic XKCD and /r9k/ robot automoderation script.
It was made in under a few hours for my friends and to train my SQL. Since I'm pretty
bad at SQL.

Code quality varies, but I tried to keep it under 100 LOC.
Features an -lb and a -stats command showing the top 10 users with the most infractions
and how many infractions YOU have.

Does not feature video/image hashing, it can easily be bypassed by prefacing the link to
an image/video with a character or message.
